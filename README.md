# IdeaVault

**Live Demo:** [hvercel.app](hvercel.app) 
IdeaVault is a community-driven platform where entrepreneurs, developers, and creators can share their startup ideas and get real-time validation from the community. Instead of keeping your next big concept a secret, you can post it here to gather feedback, see if people actually want it, and refine your business model before writing a single line of code.

## Key Features

- **Interactive Idea Board:** Browse through a clean, modern grid of startup ideas. Filter them by categories like Tech, Health, or Education, or use the search bar to find specific concepts instantly.
- **Community Validation & Engagement:** Every idea has its own dedicated discussion page. Users can leave detailed comments, edit or delete their feedback later, and upvote the concepts they love the most.
- **Personal Dashboard:** Keep track of everything you do on the platform. The "My Ideas" section lets you manage (update/delete) your own posts, while the "My Interactions" page shows a history of all the ideas you've commented on.
- **Trending Algorithm:** The homepage doesn't just show random posts. It automatically highlights the top 6 trending startup ideas based on community engagement and recent activity to help the best concepts get maximum visibility.
- **Secure Authentication & Protected Routes:** Built with robust JWT-based authentication. Users can securely register with email and password or use one-click Google login. The platform remembers your session, so you don't get logged out randomly when refreshing private pages.
- **Custom Theming:** A built-in dark/light mode toggle that saves your preference globally. The entire UI uses a premium glassmorphism design that looks great on both mobile phones and desktop screens.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend:** Next.js , React , Custom CSS 
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **Authentication:** BetterAuth (JWT based)
